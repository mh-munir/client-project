Network

Amigos Venture Capital is striving to create a community that helps entrepreneurs to thrive on their journey.

As part of our investment philosophy – Value and Vision Beyond Capital – we want to help the entrepreneur to meet the best companies, people, and organizations in the industry to enable growth and profitability.

When funded by Amigos Venture Capital you will get access to our network of companies, industry professionals and organizations that can provide anything from specific software solutions to consultancy or only a quick advice on your burning problems. The Amigos network is a key component for our success - empowering our entrepreneurs to build exceptional companies.

Telnesstech specializes in automated digital telco solutions, assisting CSPs, MVNOs, and other telecom firms in enhancing customer satisfaction and reducing operational expenses through automation. Their cloud-based BSS/OSS platform, tailored for end-to-end operations, is connectivity-ready across 64 countries, facilitating streamlined telecom operations globally. 

Launch services and outsourced operating management for B-Brands and MVNOs

Many of the tasks needed to launch a B-Brand or MVNO are essential for launch but are a reduced focus once the operation moves to Business-as-Usual mode. Examples include the licence application, platform selection, wholesale contracting, overall strategy, brand and product positioning, setting up sales and distribution channels.

A global community linking MVNOs with the intelligence, innovation, and partners essential for business growth through a holistic offering of events, digital content, and marketing services.

Their mission is to unite the industry regularly, foster collaborative discussions, share innovative solutions, and address the challenges and opportunities that define the MVNO sector.

Nordic eSIM specializes in SIM card production, data management, and eSIM personalization. They offer worldwide managed services for eSIM and subscription management, ensuring compatibility across all eSIM types. Providing a full solution package, including software platforms and customer support, Nordic eSIM is the optimal choice for operators, service providers, M2M, and IoT sectors.

The Lebara Brand Licence Partner Program supports those who launch, run and own Mobile Virtual Network Operator (MVNO) businesses around the world. They help partners achieve high returns from capital they invest in their Lebara MVNO business, at reduced risk and at pace.

Lebara serves over seven million customers across nine markets, four of which are members of the Brand Licence Partner Program. Their purpose is to consistently provide customers with a better value alternative to the established category players.

WorkBench is a boutique eSIM and MVNO consulting business with big ideals. With a wide team of associates around the world, they have the global experience for any project.
